# 📌 Project Overview

Financial institutions process thousands of loan applications every year. Making consistent lending decisions requires analyzing applicant demographics, financial information, assets, and creditworthiness.

This project simulates the role of a **Data Analyst** working for a financial institution to analyze the bank's loan approval process and build an interactive Power BI dashboard for business stakeholders.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze historical loan application data.
* Identify factors affecting loan approvals.
* Evaluate applicant financial and credit profiles.
* Define business KPIs.
* Build an executive Power BI dashboard.
* Provide actionable business recommendations.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

* Credit score is the strongest indicator of loan approval.
* Higher income alone does not guarantee loan approval.
* Moderate loan amounts represent the majority of applications.
* Customer segmentation improves lending strategy.
* Interactive dashboards enhance executive decision-making.

---

# 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Continue prioritizing credit score in loan evaluation.
* Implement multi-factor loan assessment.
* Introduce customer risk segmentation.
* Develop premium loan products.
* Improve financial education for high-risk applicants.
* Strengthen data quality governance.
