# Business Questions

## Purpose

The objective of these business questions is to guide the analytical process and ensure that the project delivers insights aligned with business objectives.

---

# Loan Approval Performance

* What is the overall loan approval rate?
* How many applications were approved and rejected?
* What is the rejection rate?

---

# Applicant Demographics

* Does gender influence loan approval?
* Does marital status affect approval decisions?
* Are graduates approved more frequently?
* Does the number of dependents influence loan approval?

---

# Financial Analysis

* How does applicant income affect loan approval?
* Does co-applicant income increase approval chances?
* Which loan amount ranges have the highest approval rate?
* Does loan amount influence approval decisions?
* Does loan term impact approval?

---

# Credit Risk Analysis

* How does credit history affect loan approval?
* Which applicant groups appear to be lower risk?
* Which characteristics are commonly associated with rejected applications?

---

# Geographic Analysis

* Which property area has the highest approval rate?
* Are applicants from Urban, Semi-Urban, or Rural areas more likely to receive loan approval?

---

# Business KPIs

The analysis will calculate the following KPIs:

* Overall Loan Approval Rate
* Loan Rejection Rate
* Average Applicant Income
* Average Loan Amount
* Average Loan Term
* Approval Rate by Education
* Approval Rate by Employment Status
* Approval Rate by Property Area
* Approval Rate by Credit History
* Gender-wise Approval Rate

---

# Expected Business Outcomes

By answering these questions, the project will:

* Improve understanding of the loan approval process.
* Identify key approval drivers.
* Reduce lending risk.
* Support evidence-based business decisions.
* Improve operational efficiency.
* Enhance customer satisfaction through fair and consistent lending practices.
