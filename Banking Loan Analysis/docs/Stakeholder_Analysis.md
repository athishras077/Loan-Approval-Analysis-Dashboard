# Stakeholder Analysis

## Overview

Stakeholders are individuals or departments that are directly or indirectly affected by the loan approval process. Understanding stakeholder requirements helps ensure the analysis addresses real business needs.

---

# Stakeholder Identification

| Stakeholder       | Role                         | Business Interest                                    |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| Loan Officers     | Review loan applications     | Faster and more consistent loan approvals            |
| Credit Risk Team  | Evaluate applicant risk      | Reduce loan defaults and financial losses            |
| Branch Managers   | Monitor branch performance   | Improve operational efficiency                       |
| Senior Management | Strategic decision making    | Increase profitability and reduce business risk      |
| Compliance Team   | Ensure regulatory compliance | Maintain fair and compliant lending practices        |
| Customers         | Apply for loans              | Receive fair, transparent, and timely loan decisions |

---

# Stakeholder Requirements

## Loan Officers

Need:

* Faster application review
* Better understanding of applicant characteristics
* Consistent approval guidelines

---

## Credit Risk Team

Need:

* Risk indicators
* Credit history analysis
* Applicant financial profiling
* Default risk insights

---

## Branch Managers

Need:

* Branch-level performance metrics
* Loan approval trends
* Operational efficiency reports

---

## Senior Management

Need:

* Executive dashboards
* Business KPIs
* Profitability insights
* Strategic recommendations

---

## Compliance Team

Need:

* Fair approval analysis
* Demographic approval comparisons
* Regulatory reporting support

---

## Customers

Need:

* Faster processing
* Transparent approval criteria
* Improved customer experience

---

# Stakeholder Expectations

The analysis should provide:

* Accurate business insights
* Reliable KPIs
* Easy-to-understand dashboards
* Actionable recommendations
* Clear documentation supporting business decisions
